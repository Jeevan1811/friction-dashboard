MASTER FORMULA
SHS = (0.35 × RE) + (0.25 × DS) + (0.25 × OE) + (0.15 × AI)
RE — Revenue Efficiency (35%)
(Actual Revenue ÷ Target Revenue) × 100
Revenue in RM is survival. Highest weight.
DS — Decision Speed (25%)
100 − (Avg Decision Days ÷ 14) × 100
Speed of iteration is your competitive advantage.
OE — Operational Efficiency (25%)
((New − Old) ÷ Old) × 100, normalised
Do more with less. Efficiency compounds.
AI Impact (15%)
((New AI Out − Old) ÷ Old) × 100, normalised
Proof your AI product is actually working.
